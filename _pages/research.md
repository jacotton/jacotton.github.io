Research
============

My background is in phylogenetics and molecular evolution, but for the last ten years, my research has focused 
on building genomic data resources for eukaryotic parasites, and then using those tools to understand various 
aspects of parasite biology. I am particularly interested in generating large-scale data on parasite genetic variation, and in understanding
parasite population genetics, evolution and epidemiology. I have worked on quite a wide range of organisms, particularly *Leishmania*, the nematodes *Haemonchus contortus* and Guinea worm (*Dracunculus medinensis*), and recently *Schistosoma*.
